Why Hello Code ?

Please read this once

According to the NEP 2020 (New Education Policy) that has been approved by the Narendra Modi-led National Democratic Alliance government, students will be taught coding as a subject from class 6th.

By keeping this point in mind, I started with this tutorial portal for the those students who started from the very beginning and have not any prior knowledge of coding. By refering this even a 6th class student can also understand in a very easy and impressive way. Our goal is to help those students who have interest in coding but doesn't have good resource. This platform is completely free and no need to charge even a single paise to learn here. We will launch YouTube tutorial also for the better understanding.

Those who want to start this journey along with me and help in building a new youth for our India is forever Welcome. My moto is only to provide free and best education to every student. Every individual has right to education irrespective of anything.
