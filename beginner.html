Let's begin code journey !

Coding is nothing but a way to communicate with our devices. It is not limited only upto our computers or laptos, coding is everywhere in our surrounding electronics devices. For example our digital watch, television remote, smart phones and a lot more things. Computers or any other other electronic devices didn't understand the language in which human communicate like hindi, english, german, spanis, french, etc. Computers and other electronics devices understand only 0 and 1. By using the combination of different 0's and 1's we communicate with devices.
Interesting thing is that computer also didn't understand 0's and 1's. For our better understanding we code the term "switch off" to 0 and "switch on" to 1. We use billions of nano electronics chips to generate electronic signals by switch off and switch on and the combination of these singals are converted into human understandable format language.
